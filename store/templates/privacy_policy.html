Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Overview

Welcome to EShop. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle the data you provide while interacting with our e-commerce prototype.

2. Information We Collect

For the purpose of this prototype, we collect limited information, including:

  • Personal identifiers (Name, Email Address).
  • Account credentials (encrypted passwords).

3. Use of Information

The data collected is used solely to demonstrate the functionality of the platform, such as maintaining your login state and simulating the checkout process.

4. Data Protection

While we use standard security protocols, this is a development environment. We advise users not to share highly sensitive personal information or reuse critical passwords.
